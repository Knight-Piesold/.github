# Knight Piésold Ltd.

Knight Piésold is a global engineering and environmental consultancy with over 100 years of experience. 
We specialize in mining, tailings and mine waste management, hydropower, water resources, and 
environmental services, with 29 offices operating across 16 countries.

This GitHub organization is maintained by KP's AI Development Team and hosts internal tools, 
data access utilities, and engineering workflow automation built to support our technical staff.

For more information, visit [knightpiesold.com](https://knightpiesold.com).
